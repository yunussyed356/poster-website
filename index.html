Keylogger

  • Avoid public/unsecured Wi-Fi; use a VPN if necessary.

    Conclusion

    Cyber security is not just a technical subject; it is a necessity for every individual and organization. Attacks like phishing, spyware, and SQL injection are becoming more advanced every day, making it important to stay alert. By learning about these threats and taking preventive measures, we can reduce risks and protect our data. Simple actions like updating passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and using secure networks go a long way in safety. Governments and companies are also investing heavily in cyber defense, but personal awareness is the first line of defense. If we all stay cautious, the digital world can be safer for everyone.
